Coral is an upscale Japanese restaurant known for its elegant ambiance and fresh seafood. Established in 2013, it offers a menu blending traditional techniques with modern innovation.

Our Ambiance

Inspired by the ocean, Coral features minimalist wooden décor and an open sushi bar, attracting both locals and international guests.
